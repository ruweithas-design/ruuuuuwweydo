Website Navigation Guide

This guide explains how visitors can use the Safa Taste Restaurant website. Each page has a clear purpose and can be opened from the navigation menu.

Home Page
The home page introduces the restaurant, popular dishes, and opening hours.
Menu Page
The menu page shows food categories, item descriptions, and prices in a table.
Gallery Page
The gallery page shows restaurant food photos in a responsive image grid.
Booking Page
The booking page allows customers to reserve a table using an online form.
About Page
The about page explains the business background, mission, and project team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our customers.

1. How can I book a table?

You can book a table by opening the Booking page and completing the reservation form.

2. Can I see the restaurant menu online?

Yes. You can open the Menu page to view food items, descriptions, and prices.

3. Does the restaurant offer family meals?

Yes. Safa Taste Restaurant offers family-friendly meals and special family deals.

4. Can I request a special table?

Yes. On the Booking page, you can write your special request in the message box.

5. Where is the restaurant located?

Safa Taste Restaurant is located in Mogadishu City Centre, Somalia.

6. What are the opening hours?

We open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Saturday is 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Sunday is 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
